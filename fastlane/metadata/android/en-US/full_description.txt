Ella, the smart and adorable dog, is lost in a jungle adventure and is doing whatever it takes to find the way out. In this free-jumping and running dog game, Ella runs automatically as the screen moves forward. Jump over obstacles and collect as many coins and gifts as you can. You have 2 objectives in this side-scrolling adventure game: 
    • avoid hitting obstacles and hurdles
    • collect coins.

Easy to learn, yet addictive and endless running dog game
Ella, the free jumping and running game, has a clean, neat design with a user-friendly and easy to use interface. The gameplay is so easy to learn that you’ll get the whole idea after jumping over obstacles and collecting coins a couple of times. There are three different levels of difficulties and as you choose more difficult levels, you need to be more precise and accurate with you jumps.
